title: "QUVAC: quantum vacuum signal calculator"
excerpt: "Quvac (from quantum vacuum, pronounced as qu-ack 🐸) allows to calculate quantum vacuum signals produced during light-by-light scattering. It uses linear Maxwell equations to describe the evolution of background electromagnetic fields and vacuum emission picture to calculate the transition amplitudes. It contains a lot of nice utilities supporting the research process (e.g. Bayesian optimization and simulations on a cluster)."
